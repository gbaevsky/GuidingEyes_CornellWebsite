Meeting Times and Events

Monthly General Body Meetings

  • Our monthly meeting to stay up-to-date on our club's activities and progress throughout the semester -- this includes activities such as puppy tick-or-treating and puppy obstacle courses
  • Location: normally in big lecture halls on campus

Pet-A-Puppy Event

  • Our combined events with other organizations on campus where we bring puppies to be pet by participants
  • Location: normally dictated by organization that we are working with

Puppy Classes

  • Observing raisers working with their dogs to develop skills under the guidance of the professionals from the National Guiding Eyes organization
  • Location: Vet School Atrium
