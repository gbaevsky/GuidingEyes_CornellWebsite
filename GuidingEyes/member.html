How to Become Involved

General Member

  • Attend our monthly General Body Meetings

Attend our On-Campus Events

  • Earn a point for attendance every time you go to an event and check in with the Eboard

Run for an Eboard Position

  • The President and Vice President positions are chosen by the Finger Lakes Regional Manager, current President, and VP, while all other positions are voted on by club members

Become a Dog Sitter

  • A handful of sitters are chosen each semester to go through the training process which includes Pre-Puppy Placement Classes, a Campus Community Workshop, and Shadow Hours with current raisers and sitters. Sitters are chosen based on their points, showing their involvement in the club, as well as their interactions with the Eboard

Become a Dog Raiser

  • One semester of sitting is required prior to becoming a raiser

Scroll down for more information on how to become a sitter or raiser

How to Become A Sitter

To become a sitter, you must show interest in our events by going to as many as you can. At the beginning of each semester, Eboard will look at the individuals who have been to the most events and have demonstrated interest in becoming a sitter. They will then go through a short interview process. Following the interview process, they will attend national training classes including Pre-Puppy Placement Class (PPPC) and a Campus Community Workshop (CCW). They must also complete 3 shadow hours with Eboard and/or Raisers.

How to Become A Raiser

An individual must first go through the entire sitter process. Its often customary to sit for an entire semester then email the Regional Manager (RM) to express interest in raising.
